Transaction Burning Mechanism
When the holder of $META tokens sells or transfers, 10% of the transfer amount will be taken as a fine.
Combustion mechanism
The $META token has a multi-dimensional combustion mechanism. When the amount of destruction exceeds the new circulation of mining in the same period, the $META token will enter a deflationary burn cycle. The scenarios that trigger the destruction of $META tokens include planet auction, NFT sale, payment of operating expenses, etc.
